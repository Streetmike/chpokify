200 icebreakers for your Agile team

Icebreaker questions are great for engaging your team members and getting them to talk. We've put together 200 icebreaker questions to make your sprint planning meetings more fun.

There are no right or wrong answers to these questions. Anyone can answer them however they want, which can give you insights into your team and make sure everyone is heard.

Here are 200 great icebreaker questions
We've highlighted our favorites in bold. Feel free to start with them if you don't have time to go through the full list.

2. What is an incredibly common thing that you've never done?
